# Design DNA -- Ghost Water Detector

## Colour Palette
- Background: #0a0a0f (near-black)
- Surface: #14141e (dark panels)
- Surface Glass: rgba(20,20,30,0.85) with backdrop-filter: blur(12px)
- Primary Accent: #00f3ff (cyan)
- Danger/Burst: #ff3333
- Warning/Illegal Tap: #ffaa00
- Info/Seepage: #22d3ee
- Success/Resolved: #22c55e
- Text Primary: #ffffff
- Text Secondary: rgba(255,255,255,0.6)

## Typography
- Headings: Orbitron (Google Fonts), weight 700-900
- Body: Rajdhani (Google Fonts), weight 300-700
- Monospace data: JetBrains Mono

## Component Style
- Cards: rounded-lg, glass background, 1px border rgba(255,255,255,0.1)
- Buttons: rounded, uppercase, letter-spacing 1px
- No generic Bootstrap/Material look -- premium, dark, utility-dashboard aesthetic
